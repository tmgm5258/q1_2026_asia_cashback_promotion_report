Key Takeaway

The funnel narrows sharply at Tier 1, then holds relatively well through Tiers 2 and 3. Of 466 sign-ups, only 138 (29.6%) completed Tier 1 — meaning over 70% of participants never met even the lowest threshold of $100 net deposit + 1 lot. Management Summary

Q1 2026 Asia Cashback Promotion — Executive Summary

The Q1 Asia Cashback Promotion ran across 5 countries (Vietnam, Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, India) from January to March 2026, attracting 466 sign-ups who generated $1.02M in first-time deposits, $4.94M in gross deposits, and $813K in net deposits at an estimated cashback cost of ~$28,730 — a 35.7x FTD-to-cashback ratio.
The promotion is a strong acquisition driver — 83.3% of participants (388) registered with TMGM during Q1. New clients contributed 86.9% of FTD ($891K) and $1.01M in positive net deposits, confirming the cashback promotion functions as an acquisition tool, not just a loyalty reward.
Vietnam dominates all value metrics, contributing 77.2% of FTD ($790K), 122.7% of net deposits ($999K), and 27 of 31 Tier 3 completions. The IB channel is the primary driver — IB-referred clients have an 82.1% FTD rate with $4,027 avg FTD, compared to 30% and $605 for LA/Marketing channels. One IB alone (IB-50047754) generated 40.5% of all FTD ($415K).
The tier structure needs recalibration. 90.9% of Tier 1 clients (298 of 328) failed to complete even the $100 + 1 lot minimum. Tier 2 has a 1.7% completion rate — the 25x jump from Tier 1 ($100) to Tier 2 ($2,500) is too steep. In contrast, Tier 3 has a 39.2% completion rate and these 79 clients drive 82.2% of net deposits. An intermediate tier between $100 and $2,500 is recommended.
Concentration risk is significant. 10 clients (2.1% of participants) account for 90.3% of net deposits — all Vietnamese Tier 3 traders. 45 Vietnamese Tier 3 clients generate more net deposits ($888K) than the entire campaign total ($813K). Additionally, 13 individuals registered with 34 accounts (potential multi-account abuse), including one person with 6 Tier 3 accounts and $215K combined FTD.
Malaysia is a concern with -$222K net deposits despite active trading (40.6K lots). Philippines and India contributed only 0.3% of combined FTD with zero tier completions. Laos, though listed as eligible, had zero participants.
Note: This is a mid-point snapshot. The Trading Lot Calculation Period extends to 30 June 2026. 38 clients are still in the extended period (deposit met, working on lot requirements). Cashback data from the Data team shows $0 across all months — flagged for investigation, estimated at $28,730 from CRM coupon data. A final report will be produced after 30 June 2026.
Recommended next steps: (1) Add intermediate tier for Q2; (2) Implement name/device/IP duplicate detection; (3) Strengthen IB partnerships, especially IB-50047754; (4) Diversify beyond Vietnam; (5) Follow up on cashback data anomaly and missing columns with Data team; (6) Final data pull after 30 June.
